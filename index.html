About Me

Hi! I'm a Customer Experience Agent at Slack, cat mom, sometimes-writer of words, and aspiring writer of code. I love writing about life, and am really excited to learn to write code, specifically in terms of web design.

I'm passionate about building community, and giving back when able to. Thus, I've co-founded a non-profit organization called Gather. Gather organizes fundraising events where women in technology can come together to meet, connect, and have a good time with others in the industry. All of our events are 100% sponsored, with proceeds going to a local women's charity in Vancouver. Sound cool? You can check out our website over here.
