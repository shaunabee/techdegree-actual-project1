What's all this about?

So, as I mentioned, I'm trying my hand at front end web development and design. This is my first project, yay! I currently work in technology at Slack as a Customer Experience Agent. I live in downtown Vancouver with my partner and our two kittens (Chip & Frank). I co-organize events for Gather, do a bit of writing, laugh a lot, and watch Netflix. That's me in a nutshell.
